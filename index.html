Late Night Coders

A Meetup for the Rest of Us

Late Night Coders is a monthly meetup in the Hampton Roads/757 area, where we get together to meet other people in the field, eat good food, and talk tech. Our group is welcoming to anyone with any interest, experience, or skill level.

We meet the first Thursday of every month, usually at Brixx in Virginia Beach.

Laptops are welcome, but don't expect to get anything done.
